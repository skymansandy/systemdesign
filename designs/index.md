## Document Structure

Every article follows a consistent flow:

1. **Scoping the Problem** — Clarifying questions, requirements, constraints
2. **API Design** — Protocol choice, key endpoints, serialization
3. **Backend / Mobile Architecture** — System diagrams, component deep-dives
4. **Scalability, Reliability & Edge Cases** — What breaks, how to handle it
5. **Wrap Up & References** — Key decisions, further reading
